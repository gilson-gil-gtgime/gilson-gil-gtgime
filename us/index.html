About me


Graduated in Economics at Insper and tech-savvy since a kid. In 2011, working at TOTVS (major ERP System from Brazil), had a lot of contact with IT people and processes and discovered my passion for programming. I decided, then, to redirect my career and learn more about programming. For a whole year I studied, took courses and started small projects. As an autodidact I googled/stackoverflowed and read books to deepen my knowledge in tools, languages and SDKs.

I started as an intern in a company (pactodigital.). There I learned a lot and it didn't took long before I got noticed and in 2013 I was invited to become a co-owner. With the technical knowledge improved and a will to make a difference, a new challenge awoke in me, open my own Startup. Resigned from my position at pactodigital and together with 3 partners, we founded doisdoissete to create solition and create value to the final user. In 2015 we were invited to present one of our solutions at CollisionConf in Vegas and later at WebSummit in Dublin.

At the end of 2016 another cycle has reached an end and I start 2017 with new challenged and adventures. In my trajectory I developed/published over 40 projects, and the most recent ones are available at my portfolio.

Taxi Fair

Taxi Fair is a simple application to compare prices of various forms of transport in your city, be it taxi, uber, lyft or cabify.

Simply enter the starting address and destination and the app will calculate prices in different platforms, considering estimated distance and time and also surge price.

- - -

This if my very first own child(app), since the conception of idea, layout, back-end development and iOS development, all by myself. After hitting over 5 thousands monthly users and even a copycat (they didn't bother to change the layout), I brought the idea to a friend Ranieri and the Android version is now live too.

Category: Own project

Equipa Esportes

Equipa Esportes was developed to help you meet a group of people to practice sports, jog after work or even kart racing in the weekends, allowing you to create your own event. In the app it is possible to find sports events in a variety over 15 different sports!

In the app you can:

Organize or find event of over 15 sports;
Possibility to setup private or public events;
Allow recurring or sporadic events;
Exclusive chat for each group;
Fave your events;
Filter by sport, date, time and distance;
Receive notifications for each event;
Change address, time, name and description at any time;
And a lot MORE!

info us pro

O info us pro is a simple tool to help teachers follow and update the number of classes each of his/her students has already paid for.

You only need to register your students with name and phone, type when the payment is made (before or after classes), how many classes was paid and even when they expire. Simple as that!

To update the number of classes available, you only need to add/subtract a class in the student details screen.

It is possible to check, through a history, when each class was taught and be always up to date about student's presence.

If the students wish to check the number of classes available, there is an app for them too.

Simple as that.
